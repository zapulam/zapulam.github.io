Bio

I started taking professional photos in 2018. Since then, I have continued to grow my photography skill-set and gain more experience taking a variety of photos. This website is meant to be a showcase of my work.

Currently, I live in downtown Nashville, TN, and work in as a Data Scientist.

Originally, I am from Cynthiana, KY. I received my bachelor's degree in physics from Transylvania University in 2020 and went on to receive my master's degree in data science from the University of Kentucky in 2022.
