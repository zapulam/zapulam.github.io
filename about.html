Here's a little about me...

I have always had a passion for art and photography and started taking professional photos in 2018. Since then I have continued to grow my love for photography and gain more experience taking a variety of photos.

Currently, I live in downtown Nashville, TN, and work in the city as a Data Scientist. While excited about creating art and photographs, I am also excited to advance my data science career and continue to grow my knowledge around machine learning and statistical analyses.

Originally, I am from Cynthiana, KY, a small rural town in central Kentucky. I recieved my bachelor's degree in physics from Transylvania University in 2020 and went on to recieve my master's degree in data science from the University of Kentucky in 2022.
